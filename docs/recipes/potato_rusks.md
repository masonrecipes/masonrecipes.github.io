# Potato Rusks

## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter or butter flavored Crisco
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup mashed potatoes
- 2 tsp salt
- 4 eggs (beaten)
- 1 cake compressed yeast
- 2 cup water
- 8 cup flower
- Topping
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 2 Tbsp flour

## Instructions

1. For the rusks, mix in the order given.
2. Put in a large greased bowl and cover.
3. Let stand for several hours.
4. Roll and cut.
5. Place in a pan and let rise for two hours.
6. Bake at 350˚ F for 15 minutes or until golden brown
7. For the topping, mix together ingredients and spread on rusks before serving.
