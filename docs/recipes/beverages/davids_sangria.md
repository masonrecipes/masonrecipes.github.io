# David's Sangria

## Ingredients

- 1 bottle of medium-grade red wine (15 dollars ish), preferably Spanish wine.
- 1 cup brandy
- 1 cup grenadine
- 2/3 cup orange juice
- 4 Tbsp of brown sugar
- 1 Orange
- 2-3 Apples
- 2-3 Pears

## Instructions

1. Slice orange and squeeze in a large serving container.
2. Slice apples and pears and place them in a pitcher.
3. Pour the bottle of wine into the pitcher.
4. Add Brandy, grenadine, orange juice, and brown sugar.
5. Stir.
6. Cover and place in the fridge for 10-20 mins.
7. When ready to serve, add ice.
