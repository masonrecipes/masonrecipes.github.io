# Texas Chocolate Sheet Cake

Given to Jan Mason from Great Grandma Mason(Ester and BF) and used for all little Mason birthdays since the beginning of time.

## Ingredients

### Cake

- 2 c flour
- 2 c sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 sticks butter (1/2 lb)
- 1 c water
- 3 T cocoa
- 2 eggs (room temp)
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 c buttermilk
- 1 tsp vanilla

### Icing

- 1/2 stick butter
- 3 T cocoa
- 6 T milk
- 1 lb powdered sugar (3 3/4c)
- 1/2 c chopped nuts
- 1/2 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

### Cake

1. Sift dry ingredients
2. Heat together water, butter and cocoa
3. Pour over dry ingredients
4. In a separate bowl, mix together eggs, buttermilk, vanilla, soda then add to the rest
5. Pour into a 9x13 cake pan (a jelly roll pan will make it flatter)
6. Bake at 350Â° for 25-30 min or until a toothpick come out clean

### Icing

1. Heat together butter, cocoa and milk on low heat
2. Remove from heat and add sugar, nuts and vanilla
3. Frost cake
