# Peach Crumb Pie

## Ingredients

### Pie Filling

- 2 1/2 Tbsp Tapioca
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 4 cup sliced fresh peaches
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice

### Topping

- 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 2 1/2 Tbsp soft butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 F.
2. Mix pie filling ingredients and let stand for 5 mins.
3. Spoon into 9 inch, unbaked pie shell.
4. Mix topping with a fork until the size of peas.
5. Sprinkle with topping and bake for 15 mins.
6. Turn heat down to 350 F and bake for 30-35 mins.
