# Mike's Quick Fajitas

## Ingredients

- 4 chicken breakers - boneless, skinless
- ½ green pepper, chopped
- ½ onion, chopped
- 3 Tbsp oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 8 oz. medium/hot salsa
- Tortillas
- Refried beans
- Cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese

## Instructions

1. Stir fry chicken, green pepper, and onion in oil.
2. Add cumin and salsa.
3. Heat for 5 min.
4. Salt to taste.
5. Serve on warmed tortillas with refried beans and cheese.
