# Tatanya with Everything

![tatanya](../../assets/tatanya_with_everything.jpeg)

## Ingredients

- 1 lb smoked sausage sliced
- 1 cup chopped onions
- 1 cup chopped apples
- 1 cup chopped broccoli

## Instructions

1. Brown the sausage and onions.
2. Add the chopped apples and brocolli halfway through browning.
3. Add packet of Ranch dressing and 1/2 cup sour cream
4. Serve over favorite pasta.
