# Mike’s Caramels

## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter or margarine
- 1, 16-ounce package (2½ cups packed) brown sugar
- 1 cup light corn syrup
- 1, 14-ounce can (1½ cups) sweetened condensed milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Generously butter a 9x9x2-inch pan; set aside.
2. In a heavy 3-quart saucepan melt the 1 cup butter or margarine.
3. Add sugar and dash salt; stir thoroughly.
4. Stir in corn syrup.
5. Gradually add condensed milk; stir constantly.
6. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, to 245° (firm-ball stage), for 15 to 20 minutes (mixture should boil gently over the entire surface).
7. Remove from heat; stir in vanilla.
8. Pour into buttered pan.
9. When cool, cut into squares with a wet, sharp knife.
10. Wrap each piece in clear plastic wrap. Makes about 2½ pounds
