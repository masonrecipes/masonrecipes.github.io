# Chipotle Fish Tacos

### Ingredients

- 1 lb oven-baked fish

- 1 ½ cup shredded sharp cheese

- ½ cup corn

- ¼ cup fresh cilantro

- Lime wedges

- 8 six-inch corn tortillas

- Sauce

- ½ cup plain yogurt

- 2 Tbsp mayo

- 2 tsp chipotle peppers in adobe sauce

### Instructions

1. Cook fish in the oven as directed on the package.

2. While the fish is cooking, create the sauce.

3. Place yogurt in a paper towel-lined strainer for 20 mins

4. Then, in a small bowl, combine yogurt, mayo, and peppers.

5. Heat tortillas on the grill for 1 min on each side.

6. Flake fish with a fork.

7. Top each tortilla with 1 Tbsp of chipotle sauce.

8. Top with fish, cabbage, corn, and cilantro.

9. Serve with lime wedges

### Tips

You can make a large batch of the sauce and store it in the refrigerator. It is great on everything!
