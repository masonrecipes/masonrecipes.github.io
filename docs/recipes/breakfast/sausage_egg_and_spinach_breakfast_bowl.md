# Sausage Egg and Spinach Breakfast Bowl

## Ingredients

- 1 cup diced smoked sausage
- 1/3 medium onion diced
- Large handful of spinach
- 5 pasture raised eggs beaten
- 2 slices sharp cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Brown sausage and onion together in bacon grease or refined coconut oil.
2. Add spinach and cover until wilted.
3. Set mixture aside.
4. Scramble eggs with cheese.
5. Serve eggs with mixture on the side.
