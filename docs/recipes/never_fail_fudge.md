# Never Fail Fudge

## Ingredients

- 2 12 oz packages of chocolate chips
- 1 pint can marshmallow cream
- 2 cups chopped nuts
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 1 Tbsp vanilla
- Pinch of salt
- 4 ½ cup sugar
- Tall can of evaporated milk

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl put chocolate, marshmallow, nuts, butter, vanilla, and salt.
2. In a large skillet, boil sugar and evaporated milk for 4 ½ minutes.
3. Pour sugar mixture over the bowl of ingredients and blend well.
4. Pour into buttered pans.
5. Cool.
