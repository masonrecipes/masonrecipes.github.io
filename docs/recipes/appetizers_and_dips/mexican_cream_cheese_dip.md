# Mexican Cream Cheese Dip

## Ingredients

- 1 c sour cream
- 1 pkg taco seasoning
- 4 oz cream cheese softened and mixed

## Instructions

1. Mix in sour cream and taco seasoning
2. Serve on plate topped with salsa and chopped Kalmata olives
3. Use pork rinds or favorite cracker.
