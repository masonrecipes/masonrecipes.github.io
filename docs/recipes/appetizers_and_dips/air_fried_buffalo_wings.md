# Air-Fried Buffalo Wings

\![buffalo wings](../../assets/chicken_wings.jpeg)

## Ingredients

- 1-2 lb Chicken Wings
- Sweet Baby Rayâ€™s Buffalo Sauce
- Pork Barrel Barbeque Dry Rub
- Meat thermometer

## Instructions

1. Defrost the chicken wings, if needed.
2. On a separate plate,  sprinkle the dry rub on each side of the wing.
3. In the air fryer, add your wings at 390 degrees Farenheit for 3 mins.
4. Open the air fryer and flip the wings for another 3 mins.
5. Rotate the wings and drizzle the buffalo sauce on the wings. Cook for 4 mins.
6. Rotate the wings, drizzle the buffalo sauce, and cook for 4 mins.
7. Continue rotating the wings, adding sauce, and cooking for 3 mins on each side until the internal temperature of the wing is 165 degrees Farenheit.
