# Randy Masonâ€™s Margarita

## Ingredients

- 6 oz Tequila
- 3 oz Triple Sec
- 6 oz Light Beer
- 6 oz Lime Aid
- Margarita Salt
- Limes

## Instructions

1. Wet margarita glass rims with a cut lime then dip in salt to coat the rim.
2. Squeeze the remaining lime into the glass.
3. Mix wet ingredients into a pitcher
4. Add crushed ice to your prepared glasses.
5. Pour and enjoy.
