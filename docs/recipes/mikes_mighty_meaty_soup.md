# Mikeâ€™s Mighty Meaty Soup

## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb chopped bacon
- 2 lb beef, cubed
- 6 large cloves crushed garlic
- 1/2 lb sausage
- 6 stalks of celery
- Half head of cauliflower
- 1 large onion
- 6-7 carrots
- 2 15 oz cans diced tomatoes
- 1 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
- 1 box organic chicken broth
- 1 Tablespoon chicken bouillon
- 1 cup Barley and/or wild rice

## Instructions

1. Brown together the bacon, beef, garlic, and sausage.
2. While browning, chop and set aside the following:
    1. Celery
    2. Cauliflower
    3. Onion
    4. Carrots
3. Add diced tomatoes, crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, and chicken bouillon to browned meat.
4. Add veggies after a 5-10 mins.
5. Bring to boil
6. Add Barley and/or wild rice
7. Bring to boil again then simmer for 20 minutes
