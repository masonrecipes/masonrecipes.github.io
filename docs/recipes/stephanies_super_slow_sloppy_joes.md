# Stephanies Super Slow Sloppy Joes

## Ingredients

- 3 lb 90% lean ground beef
- 1 cup onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic minced
- 1 1/4 cup ketchup
- 1 cup red bell pepper, chopped
- 5 Tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 cup pack brown sugar
- 3 Tablespoons vinegar
- 3 Tablespoons mustard
- 2 teaspoons chili powder

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef, onion, and garlic.
2. Drain pan of grease.
3. Combine ingredients into a crockpot.
4. Stir in beef mixture.
5. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
6. Spoon onto hamburger buns.
7. Maybe, add a playful pickle
