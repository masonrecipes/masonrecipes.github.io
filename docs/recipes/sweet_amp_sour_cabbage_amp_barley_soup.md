# Sweet and Sour, Cabbage, and Barley Soup

## Ingredients

- 1-2 tsp oil
- 1 lb stew beef
- 1 large onion
- 4 cup water or chicken broth
- 1 small cabbage, sliced
- 1 large carrot, sliced
- 1/3 cup barley
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp powdered mustard
- 1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
- 3 Tbsp brown sugar
- 3 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 can (32 oz) tomatoes with juice or 8 tomatoes quartered

## Instructions

1. Brown stew meat.
2. Add the remaining ingredients and bring the mixture to a simmer for 45-60 minutes.
3. Add more pepper, salt, brown sugar, or lemon juice to your taste.
