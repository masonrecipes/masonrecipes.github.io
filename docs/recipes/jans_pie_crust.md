## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 1/3 cup shortening (can use butter-flavored Crisco)
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 egg
- 5 Tbsp cold water
- 2 tsp vinegar

## Instructions

1. Cut shortening into flour and salt.
2. Beat egg with a fork, add water, vinegar and add flour mixture.
3. Form into a ball. Do not over mix.
4. Refrigerate for at least one hour. Makes 1 double and a single crust.
