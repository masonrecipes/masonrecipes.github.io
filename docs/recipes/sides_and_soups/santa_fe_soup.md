# Santa Fe Soup

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs hamburger
- 1 can of pinto beans
- 1 can kidney beans
- 1 can of black beans
- 2 cans diced tomatoes
- 2 cans Ro-Tel tomatoes
- 1 package ranch dressing (dry)
- 1 package taco seasoning
- 1 can white corn
- 1 can green chilies
- Onion

## Instructions

1. Brown hamburger in a large pot
2. Add all ingredients and stir
3. Simmer on low heat until done.
4. Garnish when serving with sour cream, green onions, grated cheese, and corn chips
