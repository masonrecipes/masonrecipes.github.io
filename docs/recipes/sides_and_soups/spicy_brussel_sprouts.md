# Spicy Brussel Sprouts

## Ingredients

- 1 lb Brussel Sprouts
- 1/2 lb bacon
- 6 Tbsp Gochujang
- 2 Tbsp Bachanâ€™s Japanese Barbecue Sauce

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
2. Partially, cook the bacon - get it about 60% done as it will be going in the oven
3. While the bacon is cooking, wash and halve the Brussel sprouts
4. In a bowl, mix the 3 Tbsp Gochujang and 1 Tbsp Barbecue sauce together - adjust to your liking.
5. Add half the Brussel Sprouts to the bowl and mix with the sauce.
6. On a large baking pan, place the  Brussel Sprouts mixture.
7. Repeat steps 3-5 with the remaining Brussel Sprouts.
8. When the bacon is finished, place it on a cutting board and chop it into bits. Keep some of the grease.
9. Spread bacon (and grease) on top of the baking pan
10. Bake for 40 mins.
