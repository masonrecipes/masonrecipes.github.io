# Wild Rice Soup

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup wild rice
- 1 lb bacon
- 3 Tbsp bacon drippings
- 3/4 cup celery, chopped
- 1 cup onion, chopped
- 1/3 cup chopped green pepper
- 2, 14.5 oz cans (ready to serve) chicken broth
- 4 oz can mushrooms
- 3, 10.75 oz cans cream of mushroom soup, undiluted

## Instructions

1. Wash wild rice.
2. Boil rice for 15 minutes and drain. Set aside.
3. Fry bacon until crisp.
4. Remove bacon and discard all but 3 Tbsp bacon drippings. Saut√© celery, onion, and green pepper in the drippings.
5. Cook until the onion is transparent.
6. Put in a large kettle and rice, broth, mushrooms, soup, and dice bacon.
7. Cook on low heat for 1 hour or more.
