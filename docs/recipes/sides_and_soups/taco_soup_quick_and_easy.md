# Taco Soup - Quick and Easy

## Ingredients

- 1 Rotisserie chicken
- 3 cups water
- 2 jars of salsa (whatever kind you like)
- 1 bag of frozen corn
- 1 can cannelloni beans
- 1 can low-fat chicken broth
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Pull all the meet off of the rotisserie chicken.
2. In a crock pot, combine all the ingredients.
3. Heat on high for 1 hour or for 10 contiguous minutes of boiling.
4. Place temperature on low.
5. Serve with cheese, tortilla chips or Fritos, sour cream, avocado, or anything else you like.
