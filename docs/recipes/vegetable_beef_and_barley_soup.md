# Vegetable Beef and Barley Soup

## Ingredients

- Large Onion, chopped
- 6 cloves Garlic, crushed
- 1 1/2 cup Celery, chopped
- 1-2 lb Cubed beef (small)
- Olive oil
- 1 quart Chicken Stock
- 6-7 Cubed Potatoes
- 2 large cans diced tomatoes
- 1 1/2 cup Carrots
- (Other vegetables to taste)
- 1 cup Barley
- Bay leaf
- Salt & pepper
- Rosemary (opt)

## Instructions

1. Saut√© with olive oil the chopped onion, garlic, celery, and cubed beef.
2. Add chicken stock, cubed potatoes, diced tomatoes, and carrots.
3. Bring to a light boil then add barley.
4. Reduce heat after 10-15 mins.
5. Season with bay leaf, salt and pepper, and rosemary.
6. Cook on low for 40 mins.
