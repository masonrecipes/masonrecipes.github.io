# That Good Salad

## Ingredients

- ¾ cup canola oil
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper
- 2 lbs Romaine lettuce, torn
- 2 cup chopped tomatoes
- 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
- ⅔ cup slivered toasted almonds
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 8 slices bacon, crumbled
- 1 cup Caesar Salad croutons

## Instructions

1. In a jar with a tight lid, combine oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
2. Cover and shake well.
3. Chill.
4. In a serving bowl, toss lettuce, tomatoes, Swiss cheese, almonds, Parmesan cheese, and bacon.
5. Shake dressing and pour over salad.
6. Mix the salad.
7. Add croutons and serve immediately.
