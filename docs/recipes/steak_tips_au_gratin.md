# Steak Tips au gratin

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chuck or ribeye cut into small cubes

- 1 medium onion slivered

- 1 head of garlic cloves halved

- 1/3 package cream cheese

- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Brown together onion and garlic.

2. Add meat until cooked to desired doneness

3. Melt in cream cheese
