# Chicken Curry Casserole

## Ingredients

- 2 small packages of frozen peas
- 2 cups cooked chicken, chunks
- 2 cans cream of chicken soup
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 ¼ tsp curry powder
- Mushrooms
- ½ cup sharp shredded cheese
- Croutons
- Chinese Noodles

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven at 350˚ F
2. Cook peas for 1 or 2 minutes.
3. Put in a casserole dish.
4. Add chicken.
5. Combine soup, mayonnaise, mushrooms, and curry in a separate bowl.
6. Mix and put over the chicken.
7. Add a layer of cheese.
8. Add a layer of croutons.
9. Bake for 25 minutes at 350˚ F.
10. Sever over Chinese noodles

## Tips

For croutons, toss 1 ½ cup small bread cubes over heat in 3 Tbsp of melted butter until brown.
