# My Favorite Casserole

## Ingredients

- 2 cup uncooked noodles
- 2 onions, sliced
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 cup cottage cheese
- 1 cup milk
- 1 can mushroom soup
- 1 can mushrooms
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese
- 3 eggs, beaten

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 F.
2. Cook noodles slightly then rinse and drain.
3. Saut√© onions.
4. Add meat and cook until brown.
5. Add cottage cheese.
6. In a 2 quart casserole dish, arrange a layer of noodles, then add meat mixture.
7. Combine milk, mushroom soup, salt, and mushrooms.
8. Pour over casserole.
9. Sprinkle cheese on top.
10. Pour beaten eggs over.
11. Bake for 1 hour.
