# Taco Seasoning

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Garlic powder

- 1 teaspoon Onion powder

- 1 teaspoon Oregano

- 1 teaspoon Black pepper

- 4 Tablespoon Chili powder

- 1 Tablespoon Paprika

- 1 Tablespoon Salt

- 2 Tablespoons Cumin

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients together!
