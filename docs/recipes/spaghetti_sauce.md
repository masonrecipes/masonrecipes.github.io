# Spaghetti Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 lb ground sausage
- 1 medium onion chopped
- 3 cloves garlic minced

- 1 large can tomato sauce
- 2 heaping teaspoons tomato paste
- Oregano
