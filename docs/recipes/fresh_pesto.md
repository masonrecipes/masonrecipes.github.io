# Fresh Pesto

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup basil, chopped
- 1/4 cup toasted pistachios, chopped (can use pine nuts)
- 1 Tablespoon EVOO
- 1 Tablespoon of grated parmesan
- 2 Tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients together.
2. Add parmesan and salt to taste.
3. Service over vegetables or as an easy dip!
