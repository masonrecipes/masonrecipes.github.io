# Chicken Crust Pizza

## Ingredients

- Can of Chicken
- 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
- Basil
- Oregano
- Salt
- 1 Egg
- Your favorite pizza toppings

## Instructions

1. Mix together the chicken, Parmesan, and egg.
2. Season with basil, oregano, and salt.
3. Spread this mixture out on a baking sheet or small plate (reminder, if you are adding this to an air fryer, make sure you do this on a small plate)
4. Bake for 15 minutes at 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
5. Add your favorite pizza toppings.
6. Bake again until toppings are cooked to your desired cripsness.
