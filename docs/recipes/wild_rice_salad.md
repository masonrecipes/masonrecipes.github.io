# Wild Rice Salad

## Ingredients

- 3 cups cooked wild rice
- ½ cup craisin
- 1 small can sliced water chestnuts
- ½ lb fresh mushroom, sliced
- 2 ribs celery, diced
- 1 bunch green onion, diced.
- 1 red pepper, diced fine.
- 2 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped.
- ¼ cup grated carrot
- ½ cup or more Marzetti’s Raspberry dressing.
- 2 Tbsp red wine vinegar

## Instructions

1. Combine all salad ingredients.  Mix well
2. Add dressing until just coated.
3. Stir in vinegar.
4. Cover and chill for 1 hour.
5. Stir before serving.  Add more dressing if needed.
