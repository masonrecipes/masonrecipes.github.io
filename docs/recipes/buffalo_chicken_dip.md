# Buffalo Chicken Dip

## Ingredients

- 2 - 10 oz. cans chicken (drained)
- 2 - 8 oz. packages softened cream cheese
- 1 cup ranch or blue cheese dressing
- ¾ cup hot sauce
- 1 ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat chicken and hot sauce in a skillet on medium heat.
2. Stir in cream cheese and dressing until warm and blended.
3. Mix in ½ shredded cheese and transfer to a slow cooker.
4. Sprinkle on the remaining cheese and cover.
5. Cook on low till hot and bubbly.
6. Serve with celery and crackers.
