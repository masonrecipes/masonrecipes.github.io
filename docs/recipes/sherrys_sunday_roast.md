# Sherry's Sunday Roast

## Ingredients

- 4 lb Chuck Roast
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
- 1/2 Teaspoon Pepper
- 2 Tablespoons Oil
- 1 large onion in eighths
- 2 tablespoons beef base
- 3 tablespoons mustard
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire
- 4 teaspoons minced garlic

## Instructions

1. Coat the roast with flour, salt, pepper, and oil.
2. Brown in the skillet. Keep the drippings!
3. In a slow cooker, combine the roast, onion, beef base, mustard , Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, and drippings.
4. Fill with water until roast is covered.
5. Add seasoning to taste (we use Shoups).
6. Cook on High for 2-3 hours the reduce to Low for 2-4 hours.
