# Spicy Banana Cake or Muffins

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 3/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1/2 tsp ground cloves
- 2/3 cup shortening
- 2/3 cup buttermilk (if you do not have this, use 1 cup milk with 1 Tbsp vinegar)
- 1 1/4 cup mashed bananas
- 2 eggs, unbeaten

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
2. Sift dry ingredients.
3. Add shortening, buttermilk, and bananas.
4. Mix until flour is dampened.
5. Beat 2 mins on low speed.
6. Add eggs and beat for 1 min.
7. Pour 2, 9-inch loaf pans, greased, floured.
8. Bake for 30-35 mins.
9. For banana muffins:
    1. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full.
    1. Bake for 18 mins.
