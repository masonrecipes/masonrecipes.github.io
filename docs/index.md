# Mason Recipes

We all have to eat. Why not do it beautifully?

## Quick Start

Browse recipes by category using the navigation menu on the left, or use the search bar at the top to find specific recipes by name.

## Recipe Categories

- **Appetizers & Dips** - Buffalo wings, dips, queso, and more
- **Main Courses** - Curries, tacos, casseroles, and roasted meats
- **Sides & Soups** - Vegetables, salads, and hearty soups
- **Desserts** - Pies, cookies, cakes, and candy
- **Beverages** - Margaritas, sangria, and refreshing drinks
- **Sauces & Condiments** - Marinades, pesto, and hot sauces
- **Breakfast** - Casseroles, brunch favorites, and more
- **Breads & Extras** - Homemade breads, nuts, and specialty items
