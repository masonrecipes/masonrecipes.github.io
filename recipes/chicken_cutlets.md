# Chicken Cutlets

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Panko

- Greek yogurt

- 1/4 cup nutritional yeast

- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. First coat chicken in plain Greek yogurt on both sides.

3. Mix the Panko, Parmesan cheese, and yeast together in a separate bowl.

4. Coat the yogurt covered chicken in the dry mixture.

5. Place the chicken on a pan and cook for 20 mins.
