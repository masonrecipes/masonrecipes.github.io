# Sausage Brunch Casserole

## Ingredients

- 1 package froze hash browns

- 1 lb sausage and/or 1/2 lb bacon, cooked and drained

- 2 1/2 slices bread (cut in 1/2‚Äù cubes)

- 5 oz cheddar cheese

- 6 eggs

- 2 cups milk

- 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard

- 1 onion, chopped

- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 F

2. Butter an 8x12 baking dish

3. Put hash browns and bread on bottom.

4. Spread the meat and cheese over the hash brown-bread mixture.

5. In a mixing bowl, blend eggs, milt, salt, pepper, and dry mustard.

6. Pour over top of the contents of the baking dish.

7. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes.
