# Strawberry Salad

## Ingredients

- 2 small packages frozen strawberries or raspberries (ensure they are frozen hard)

- 2 small packages strawberry jello

- 1 1/2 cup hot water

- 2 bananas, mashed

- 1 small can crushed pineapple, well-drained

- 1 cup sour cream

- 2-3 Tbsp lemon juice

- Knox Gelatin (just a little)

## Instructions

1. Mix jello and hot water

2. Chop frozen berries into large chunks and add to jello.

3. Stir until all the ice is melted.

4. Add lemon juice and Knox Gelatin

5. Add banana and pineapple

6. Pour half of this mixture into a pan or mold.

7. Daub the sour cream over this.

8. Pour the remainder of the jello mixture on top of the sour cream.

9. This begins to set almost before you are through with the mixing
