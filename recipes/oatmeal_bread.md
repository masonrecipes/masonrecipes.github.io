# Oatmeal Bread

## Ingredients

- 1 package yeast (2 1/4 tsp)

- 1 cup cooking oats

- 3 cups breast flour

- 1 tsp salt

- 1/2 cup molasses

- 1 Tbsp vegetable oil

- 1 1/4 plus 1 Tbsp warm water

### Instructions

1. In your bread machine, add the ingredients in order.

2. Start your machine, and let it finish.

3. Enjoy!
