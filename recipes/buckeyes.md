# Buckeyes

## Ingredients

- 3 lbs powdered sugar

- 2 lbs peanut butter

- 1 lb oleo or butter

- 2 Tbsp vanilla

- 3-4 large Hershey bars

- A small amount of paraffin

## Instructions

1. Mix powdered sugar, peanut butter, oleo/butter, and vanilla.

2. Roll into small balls.

3. Refrigerate overnight.

4. Melt the Hershey bars and paraffin.

5. Dip the balls in the chocolate leaving just enough of the filling showing to make them look like buckeyes.
