# Crock Pot Potato Soup

## Ingredients

- 49.5 oz can of chicken broth
- 2 lbs frozen hash browns
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 8 oz of cream cheese

## Instructions

1. In a crock pot, combine the chicken broth, hash browns, and cream of chicken soup.
2. Set crock pot on low for 5 hours.
3. Put cream cheese in the soup and stir until blended.
4. Cook for another hour.
5. Serve soup topped with cheese and bacon.
